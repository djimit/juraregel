# AI Liability Directive → NEDERUS Crosswalk

Maps AI Liability Directive (EU 2024/2853) articles to NEDERUS unified controls.

**Important note**: The AI Liability Directive amends the Product Liability Directive
to explicitly cover AI systems. It introduces a "presumption of causality" for AI-related
damage claims. Deadline for national implementation: December 2026.

## Key Defense Strategy

The AI Liability Directive creates a "presumption of causality" — the defendant
must prove their AI system did NOT cause the damage. The NEDERUS controls provide
the evidence trail needed for this defense:

1. **NED-08**: Evidence preservation (logging, record-keeping)
2. **NED-03**: Human oversight documentation (proves human was in control)
3. **NED-02**: Bias testing (proves system was tested for fairness)
4. **NED-06**: Secure development (proves system was built securely)
5. **NED-04**: Transparency (proves users were informed)
