# Digital Services Act → NEDERUS Crosswalk

Maps DSA (EU 2022/2065) articles to NEDERUS unified controls.

**Important note**: DSA applies to "intermediary services" — platforms, hosting providers,
and online marketplaces. Most government AI systems are not platforms. This crosswalk
is relevant for organizations operating platforms with AI-driven features.

## Transparency Obligations (Art. 13-15)

| DSA Article | Requirement | NEDERUS Control | Relation |
|-------------|-------------|-----------------|----------|
| Art. 13 | Notice and action mechanisms | NED-05 (Incident Response) + NED-07 (Platform Transparency) | partial |
| Art. 14 | Out-of-court dispute settlement | — | gap (legal process) |
| Art. 15 | Trusted flaggers | NED-07 (Platform Transparency) | partial |

## Recommender Systems (Art. 27)

| DSA Article | Requirement | NEDERUS Control | Relation |
|-------------|-------------|-----------------|----------|
| Art. 27 | Transparency of recommender parameters | NED-02 (Bias & Fairness) + NED-04 (Transparency) + NED-07 (Platform Transparency) | equivalent |

## Data Access (Art. 39-40)

| DSA Article | Requirement | NEDERUS Control | Relation |
|-------------|-------------|-----------------|----------|
| Art. 39 | Data access for vetted researchers | NED-04 (Transparency) + NED-07 (Platform Transparency) | partial |
| Art. 40 | Data preservation | NED-08 (AI Liability) | partial |

## Very Large Platforms (Art. 33-34)

| DSA Article | Requirement | NEDERUS Control | Relation |
|-------------|-------------|-----------------|----------|
| Art. 33 | Systemic risk assessment | NED-01 (AI Impact Assessment) | partial |
| Art. 34 | Risk mitigation measures | NED-03 (Human Oversight) + NED-07 (Platform Transparency) | partial |

## Coverage Summary

**DSA coverage: 6/8 mapped articles (75%)**

Unmapped articles are legal processes (dispute settlement) or VLOP-specific
requirements outside NEDERUS v2.0 scope.
