# NORA → NEDERUS Crosswalk

Maps NORA (Nederlandse Overheid Referentie Architectuur) grondslagen to NEDERUS unified controls.

NORA is a reference architecture, not a compliance framework. Coverage focuses
on architectural principles relevant to AI governance.

## Grondslagen (Foundational Principles)

| NORA Grondslag | Principle | NEDERUS Control | Relation |
|----------------|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| Grondslag-toets | Assess whether AI use is legally permitted | NED-01 (AI Impact Assessment) | equivalent |
| Evenredigheid | AI use must be proportionate | NED-02 (Bias & Fairness) | partial |
| Proportionaliteit | Human oversight proportional to risk | NED-03 (Human Oversight) | equivalent |
| Openbaarheid | Transparency about AI decision-making | NED-04 (Transparency) | equivalent |
| Verantwoordelijkheid | Clear accountability for AI systems | NED-01, NED-03 | partial |
| Bescherming persoonsgegevens | Data protection in AI | NED-01 (AI Impact Assessment) | partial |
| Kwaliteit van dienstverling | AI service quality | NED-02 (Bias & Fairness) | partial |

## Coverage Summary

**NORA coverage: 7/7 grondslagen mapped (100%)**

NORA grondslagen are high-level principles that map well to NEDERUS controls.
However, NORA is not enforceable — it provides architectural guidance, not
compliance obligations. NEDERUS operationalizes NORA principles into auditable
controls.
