# Human Gates

All human interactions are intentionally deferred until autonomous technical work and validation are complete.

1. Jurist-acceptatie metadata for production-ready status: reviewer name, review date, valid-until date and accepted JREM version.
2. Approval for authenticated endpoints if public endpoints are insufficient.
3. Approval for publication of machine-readable Woo/STTR/interoperability reports on behalf of an organisation.
4. Approval to commit and push the full implementation.
