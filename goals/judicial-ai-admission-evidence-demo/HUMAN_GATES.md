# Human End Gates

No human decision below is assumed or simulated by the autonomous build.

1. [ ] Independent jurist review: confirm the profile interpretation, evidence
   mapping and use of `blocked`, `review-required` and
   `conditionally-admissible`.
2. [ ] Judicial-governance review: confirm that the demonstration preserves
   judicial authority, access to a human judge, equality of arms and effective
   remedy.
3. [ ] Security review: confirm that the evidence adapter and future runtime
   enforce source isolation, least privilege and separate authorisation.
4. [ ] Publication decision: approve or reject deployment through GitHub Pages
   and/or integration into Djimit.nl, after visual browser acceptance of the
   standalone page.
5. [ ] Commercial decision: approve or reject the Readiness & Evidence Sprint
   name, price experiment and design-partner outreach.
6. [ ] Git decision: approve or reject commit, push and pull-request creation.

Until gates 1 and 2 are recorded with reviewer, role, date, scope and profile
version, the profile and demo remain `L1-poc`, `draft` and non-production.
